Welcome!

Here are some instructions and things that can be done:

➊Pick the color of your choice.

❷When clicking on "Random Color", the color is random each time you draw.

❸Adjust the grid size via slider.

❹Reset grid by pressing button "Reset Grid".

❺Draw by clicking LMB on individual square.

❻Continiously draw by holding LMB and moving the mouse.

❼Erase by clicking RMB on individual square.

➑Continiously erase by holding RMB and moving the mouse.


Be aware that your painting will disappear if you adjust the grid size.

Enjoy!
